Organic Ice Cubes

Simple Ingredients. Simple Steps. Simply Organic.

Fresh! Frozen! Fantastic! 100% Organic Ice Cubes

You can finally chill your refreshments without chilling your expectations!

Try out this super easy, 100% Organic Ice Cube recipe made with our very own 100% Organic Boiled Water!

Ingredients

  • 100% Organic Boiled Water (cooled to room temperature)
  • Your favorite ice cube tray
  • A working freezer

Steps

  1. Fill your ice cube tray with some delicious 100% Organic Boiled Water.
  2. Find the perfect spot in your freezer and let it chill for 6-8 hours.
  3. Prepare a refreshing drink and elevate it to the next level of organic with one or two fresh cubes.
